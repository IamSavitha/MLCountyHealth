Implications

This analysis demonstrates that food desert characteristics have measurable impact on county-level metabolic health outcomes, independent of traditional socioeconomic factors. The strong predictive performance of Food_Access_Barrier_Index suggests that improving food access could be an effective public health intervention strategy.
